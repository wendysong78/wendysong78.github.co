About Us

We are a Brisbane based company well regarded locally for managing children’s optical needs with a professional and caring approach. We have two full-time, in-store qualified optometrists ready to assist with a variety of eye-related problems, striving to achieve the best visual outcomes for our customers in work, study, sport, and leisure.

Services

  • Comprehensive Eye Examinations: Including bulk-billed eye exams, diabetic eye examinations, and tests for Macular Degeneration, Glaucoma, eye pressure, and Cataracts.
  • Vision Assessment Services: Covering Colour Vision assessments, clearness of vision (myopia, hyperopia, astigmatism), focusing ability (Presbyopia), and eye muscle movement and control.
  • Spectacles and Eyewear: Offering a range of spectacles (bulk-billed to private Health Fund optical cover) and advice on the correct fitting of quality sunglasses, both prescription and non-prescription, for adults and children in a sporting environment.
  • Contact Lens Services: Specialized in contact lens fitting and management, catering to various eye conditions.
  • Advanced Eye Care: Providing referrals for eye laser surgery and specialized services for amblyopia (lazy eye).
  • Industrial and Safety Eyewear Solutions: Including Mining & Industrial Safety Spectacles, ensuring eye safety in demanding work environments.
